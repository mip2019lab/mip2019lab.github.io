MIP Lab

Medical Image Processing Laboratory

MIP Lab (Head: Fei Ma ORCID) is part of the Advanced Medical Image Processing Team of School of Computer Science, Qufu Normal University, with 5 key team members, 3 Ph.D.s and 2 senior titles. There exist 5 students currently pursuing master's degree and 4 new students. The team focuses on medical imaging technology, image acquisition, reconstruction algorithms, intelligent processing, and image retrieval and recognition, and is committed to achieving high-quality acquisition and in-depth analysis of medical images.

Teams

The Medical Image Processing Laboratory (Head: Fei Ma ORCID) is part of the Advanced Medical Image Processing Team of the School of Computer Science, which has 5 key members, 4 PhDs and 2 senior members. The team focuses on medical imaging methods, image data acquisition, reconstruction, intelligent processing and image retrieval and recognition, and is dedicated to the acquisition, recognition and mining of high quality medical images. The team members currently work closely with the Intelligent Information Processing Laboratory of Xi'an University of Electronic Science and Technology (XUEST), the Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology (SIAT) of the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), the School of Biomedical Engineering of the Fourth Military Medical University (FMMU), and the Shanghai University of Applied Sciences (SUST) in terms of data sharing and academic exchanges. Members of the group are currently leading or undertaking the National Natural Science Foundation of China, the Provincial Outstanding Young and Middle-aged Scientists Scientific Research Incentive Fund, the Shandong Provincial Science and Technology Tackling Programme and other scientific research projects at all levels more than 10, with funding of more than 1. 3 million yuan, published more than 200 papers in the domestic and international important academic journals and conferences, among which the important scientific research results published in the IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging, Optics Express, Optics Letters, IEEE Transactions on Nuclear Science, Journal of Biophotonics, Journal of Biomedical Optics and other internationally renowned SCI journals.
